For Buyers

Bharvest supports international buyers sourcing food and grocery products from India with structured coordination and visibility.

What We Provide

  • Manufacturer shortlisting based on buyer requirements
  • Sample and specification coordination
  • Third-party testing alignment
  • Documentation readiness review

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not act as exporter of record
  • We do not hold buyer funds
  • We do not guarantee regulatory approvals

Buyer Safety & Transparency

Buyers are encouraged to:

  • Conduct importer-side due diligence
  • Use secure payment mechanisms (e.g., verified LC, escrow)
  • Request third-party inspection where needed
  • Engage independent inspectors, labs, and freight forwarders of their choice

Important: Bharvest does not handle buyer payments or act as intermediary for funds. All financial arrangements should be made directly between buyer and manufacturer using buyer-controlled payment methods.
