Quality & Compliance Approach

Export quality is not a document — it is a process.

Bharvest follows a compliance-aware coordination model that aligns manufacturers, buyers, and independent service providers.

Our Coordination Process

  1. Manufacturer Pre-Screening
    Business legitimacy, product clarity, and readiness review.
  2. Sample & Specification Alignment
    Buyer-specific sampling, ingredient confirmation, and packaging review.
  3. Third-Party Testing Coordination
    Independent laboratory testing where required by buyers or destination markets.
  4. Pre-Shipment Readiness
    Documentation checklist alignment and shipment file review.

Responsibility Clarity

Bharvest Coordinates:

  • Readiness assessment
  • Testing and documentation workflows
  • Buyer-manufacturer alignment

Manufacturers Remain Responsible For:

  • Product quality and consistency
  • Regulatory compliance
  • Accuracy of documents and declarations
