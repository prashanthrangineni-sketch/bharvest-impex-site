For Manufacturers

Bharvest works with a curated network of Indian manufacturers seeking structured access to international buyers.

Important Clarification:

We do not guarantee exports. We help manufacturers become buyer-ready.

Who Should Apply

  • Spice and food manufacturers
  • Packaged grocery producers
  • Private-label capable units
  • Export-aspiring or export-experienced companies

How Enrollment Works

  1. Expression of Interest submission
  2. Pre-screening for readiness and fit
  3. Conditional onboarding based on buyer requirements

Enrollment does not guarantee buyer orders and is conditional on buyer requirements.

What We Look For

  • Business legitimacy
  • Product clarity and consistency
  • Transparency and responsiveness
  • Willingness to align packaging and documentation

Certifications (FSSAI, ISO, HACCP, Organic) are beneficial but not mandatory at entry.

Important Clarifications

  • Bharvest does not certify products
  • Bharvest does not purchase inventory
  • Bharvest does not promise buyer orders
  • Bharvest does not act as exporter of record

Manufacturers remain fully responsible for product quality, regulatory compliance, and accuracy of all documentation and declarations.
